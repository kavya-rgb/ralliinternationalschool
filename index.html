School Overview
Ralli International School is a twelve years old young premier school situated in Indirapuram. The school was established in the year 2008, a dream visualized and materialized under the guidance of Shri. Surinder Kumar Ralli, who is a pioneer in the field of Education.The school provides a conducive environment to the young minds "where the mind is without fear and the head is held high". We have been devoted and committed to furnishing qualitative education to the students to ensure their wholesome development. We firmly believe that every child is phenomenal and we aim to empower these young learners through a holistic education that encompasses a broad spectrum of application and value-based learning. The perpetual energy, movement & enthusiasm permeate the ambience at RIS.
We at Ralli International School perceives Education as a process that takes along students, educators, and parents on an intriguing and enriching journey - exploring the worlds of intellectual, emotional and spiritual learning. We set sail to cultivate Knowledge, Aptitude, Social Skills, and Moral Values amalgamating traditional values with modern methods of learning. This approach enables us to unravel and unleash one’s potential.
We never rest on our laurels, but work constantly and consistently to improvise our standards, processes, and technologyand teaching mechanisms to make sure that our students receive the best education for life. We embrace technology and use it as a potent tool and ensure that Education persistently reaches the students transcending the four walls of the classroom. Our educators do not leave any stones unturned to make learning a lifelong and enriching experience for young learners.
Our pedagogy is child-centric with emphasis on inquiry and project-based learning.Sharing ideas, analyzing situations, and expressing them confidently are essential skills that are honed here, through multifarious Experiential Learning activities that are conducted by teacher mentors at a timely interval.
The school has a clear objective of transforming each student into a responsible being thus truly upholding the motto ‘Beyond the Ordinary’.
Come; join us in our quest to develop learners who are successful and confident and who can contribute in significant ways to build a prosperous nation.
We hone the wings of your child which may carry them far and wide.
Visionary

Visionary

Here is a man who not only dreams but makes them a reality on earth. His dream is to provide global quality education based on rich Indian educational system from Vedic era with the help of latest methodology and technology. He started this venture at the age of 66 years‚ when an average man thinks of a retired life. With only two students in temporary structure of four rooms‚ he has built a beautiful building within a time span of four years where now hundreds of students are getting world class education under his leadership and patronage. He is spearheading IQ‚ EQ and SQ classes along with the faculty members to infuse true Indian values in the students. He is a living legend who works for more than 12 hours a day throughout the year. He has an educational map of what should be taught to the students of today and the next generation.
He is none other than our respected Sir‚ Shri Surinder Kumar Ralli‚ the founder of Ralli International Schools. He has lived an eventful life. He did his graduation and Post-Graduation from Kirori Mal College‚ a prestigious college of Delhi University. He was a well known writer‚ orator and debater during his college days and he was also elected Chairman of the College Union in 1963-1964. He edited a popular student news magazine "Youth Mirror" for five years. He is a law graduate from Delhi University and a member of Bar Council of India. He successfully edited "Desh Bhakt Mail"‚ a Hindi Magazine for several years. Being a staunch follower of Arya Samaj and Director of ‘Arya Vidya Parishad’ he has written a book for teachers ‘Naitik Shiksha Aur Vyavyhar Kushalta’ and twelve books for Class 1 to class 12 for the students titled ‘Shishtachar and Naitik Shiksha’ which are prescribed books in over one Thousand schools run by DAV‚ Arya Samaj and Gurukuls. These books are very popular and have been reprinted 8 times. He is also the founder of Mother Teresa Public School (CBSE Affiliated)‚ Preet Vihar‚ Delhi in 1984 and has conducted more than 900 workshops for teachers and students in various schools. Indeed he is a multi faceted personality and we are proud to have such a great visionary as our leader.
Sir‚ it is a great opportunity for all of us to be associated with such an organisation and share your vision and try our best to realise your mission.
